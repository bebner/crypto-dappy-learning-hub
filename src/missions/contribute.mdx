No matter if you're an open source veteran or newbie, CryptoDappy welcomes everyone from
everywhere to contribute, improve, extend and maintain.

<YoutubeEmbed embedId={"pCj9YkyECFU"} />

We are actively looking for developers who are wanting to contribute and/or take
ownership in some way for CryptoDappy and its learning hub, especially in the following areas:

* Internationalization of learning hub content (text, videos)
* Additional features to further improve CryptoDappy
* Improvement on current design of the application

To start contributing today, read our 
[contribution guidelines](https://github.com/bebner/crypto-dappy/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md) 
and head over to our main repositories to raise issues or propose a pull request:

* [CryptoDappy](https://github.com/bebner/crypto-dappy)
* [CryptoDappy Learning Hub](https://github.com/bebner/crypto-dappy-learning-hub)

Under the 'issues' tab, you'll already find various good first issues that are reasonably 
scoped and good for anyone wanting to dive deeper into Crypto

To get in touch with your fellow contributors and the broader CryptoDappy community,
feel free to start a conversation in the dedicated 
[CryptoDappy community forum](https://forum.onflow.org/c/community-projects/cryptodappy).
